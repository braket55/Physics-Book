Worksheet Exercise 3.

A grain of sand can be about 0.0012 meters in diameter. Convert 0.0012 to scientific notation.
Hint.
The decimal point would need to move to the right by 3 places.
Answer.
\(1.2 \times 10^{-3}\)
Solution.
To write 0.0012 in scientific notation, move the decimal point three places to the right to place it after the first nonzero digit. This gives "1.2". Since the decimal moved right by three places, the exponent on 10 is -3.
So, \(0.0012 = 1.2 \times 10^{-3}\text{.}\)
