Worksheet Exercise 1.

The influenza virus can have a diameter of about 0.000000099 meters. Convert 0.000000099 to scientific notation.
Hint.
The decimal point would need to move to the right by 8 places.
Answer.
\(9.9 \times 10^{-8}\)
Solution.
To write 0.000000099 in scientific notation, move the decimal point eight places to the right to place it after the first nonzero digit. This gives "9.9". Since the decimal moved right by eight places, the exponent on 10 is -8.
So, \(0.000000099 = 9.9 \times 10^{-8}\text{.}\)
